<p><p>The mesh fixation device market comprises two primary types: absorbable and non-absorbable fixation devices. Absorbable fixation devices are designed to gradually dissolve in the body, reducing the risk of long-term complications and the need for additional surgeries. In contrast, non-absorbable fixation devices remain permanently implanted, offering durable support for mesh materials used in surgical procedures such as hernia repairs. Both types play critical roles in enhancing surgical outcomes and patient recovery in various medical applications.</p></p>
