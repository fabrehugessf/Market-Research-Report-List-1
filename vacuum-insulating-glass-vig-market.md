<p><p>Vacuum Insulating Glass (VIG) is a high-performance glazing solution that uses a vacuum seal between glass panes to achieve superior thermal insulation. The market is primarily divided into two types: tempered VIG and non-tempered VIG. Tempered VIG is heat-treated for enhanced strength and safety, making it suitable for high-stress applications. In contrast, non-tempered VIG offers a cost-effective solution for general applications where extreme strength is less critical. Both types are increasingly popular in energy-efficient construction.</p></p>
