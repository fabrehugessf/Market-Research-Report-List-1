<p><p>The dicing machine market for semiconductor wafers primarily comprises two types: dicing saws and laser saws. Dicing saws use a rotating blade to slice silicon wafers into smaller chips, offering precision and efficiency for traditional applications. In contrast, laser saws employ focused laser beams to cut wafers, allowing for minimal mechanical stress and the ability to work with more delicate materials. Both technologies cater to the growing demand for miniaturization and high-performance semiconductor components in various industries.</p></p>
