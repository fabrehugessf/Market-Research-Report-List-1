<p><strong>The Cooking Thermometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Instant-Read</li><li>In-Probe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cooking thermometer market primarily features two types: instant-read and in-probe thermometers. Instant-read thermometers provide quick temperature readings, ideal for grilling or roasting, allowing users to check the doneness of meats without prolonged exposure to heat. In-probe thermometers, on the other hand, are designed to stay in the food while it cooks, providing continuous temperature monitoring. This type is particularly useful for slow-cooking methods, ensuring precise cooking results and enhancing food safety by preventing undercooked meals.</p></p>
