<p><p>Team collaboration tools are categorized into two primary markets: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. These tools facilitate remote work and real-time collaboration, making them increasingly popular among organizations seeking flexibility and cost-efficiency in their teamwork strategies.</p></p>
