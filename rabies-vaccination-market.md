<p><p>The rabies vaccination market is segmented into pre-exposure and post-exposure applications. Pre-exposure prophylaxis is administered to individuals at high risk, such as veterinarians and animal handlers, to prevent rabies infection before potential exposure. In contrast, post-exposure prophylaxis is critical for individuals who have been bitten or exposed to a rabid animal, providing immediate protection through vaccination and rabies immunoglobulin administration. Both applications are essential for controlling rabies and ensuring public health safety.</p></p>
