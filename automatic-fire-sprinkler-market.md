<p><strong>The Automatic Fire Sprinkler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glass Bubbles</li><li>Fusible Alloy</li><li>Stent Type</li><li>Heat Sink Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic fire sprinkler market features various types designed for specific conditions. Glass bubble sprinklers contain a liquid-filled glass bulb that shatters at certain temperatures, releasing water. Fusible alloy sprinklers use a heated metal alloy that melts at a designated temperature, activating the sprinkler. Stent type sprinklers employ a mechanical stent that releases water when heat triggers its release mechanism. Heat sink type sprinklers absorb heat to activate, allowing for timely water discharge during a fire event, optimizing safety and protection.</p></p>
