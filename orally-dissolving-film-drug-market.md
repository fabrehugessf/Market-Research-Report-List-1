<p><p>The orally dissolving film drug market comprises primarily two types: orodispersible films and oromucosal films. Orodispersible films rapidly dissolve in the mouth, delivering medication quickly and without the need for water, making them ideal for patients with swallowing difficulties or those seeking fast relief. Oromucosal films are designed for systemic absorption through the oral mucosa, targeting specific sites to enhance bioavailability. Both types ensure convenience, improved adherence, and a pleasant alternative to traditional dosage forms, catering to diverse patient needs.</p></p>
