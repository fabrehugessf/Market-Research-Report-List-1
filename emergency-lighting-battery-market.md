<p><strong>The Emergency Lighting Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NiCd Battery</li><li>NiMH Battery</li><li>Li-Ion Battery</li><li>Lead-Acid Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The emergency lighting battery market consists of various types, each with distinct characteristics. Nickel-Cadmium (NiCd) batteries are durable and have a long lifespan, ideal for high-drain applications. Nickel-Metal Hydride (NiMH) batteries offer better capacity and less toxicity than NiCd, making them environmentally friendlier. Lithium-Ion (Li-Ion) batteries are lightweight, have high energy density, and require minimal maintenance, while Lead-Acid batteries are cost-effective and widely used but are bulkier and have a shorter cycle life. Each type caters to specific lighting needs in emergencies.</p></p>
