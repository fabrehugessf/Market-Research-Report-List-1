<p><p>The analog oscilloscope market is categorized into single-channel and dual-channel types. Single-channel oscilloscopes measure one signal at a time, making them suitable for basic applications where only a single waveform is analyzed. In contrast, dual-channel oscilloscopes can simultaneously display two signals, allowing for detailed comparisons and the study of relationships between waveforms. This dual capability enhances versatility in troubleshooting and design work, appealing to more advanced users and applications requiring simultaneous signal analysis.</p></p>
