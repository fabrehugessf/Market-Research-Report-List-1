<p><p>The automotive retractable towbar market features two primary types: detachable towbars and fixed towbars. Detachable towbars offer convenience as they can be removed when not in use, enhancing vehicle aesthetics and reducing drag. They are ideal for occasional towing needs. In contrast, fixed towbars are permanently mounted and provide a robust solution for regular towing, often favored by those who frequently transport trailers or equipment. Both types serve distinct customer preferences based on usage frequency and vehicle design.</p></p>
