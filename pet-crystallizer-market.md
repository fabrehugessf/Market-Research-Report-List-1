<p><strong>The PET Crystallizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot-air Hopper Crystallizers</li><li>Infrared Drum (IRD) Crystallizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PET crystallizer market features various technologies, including Hot-air Hopper Crystallizers and Infrared Drum (IRD) Crystallizers. Hot-air Hopper Crystallizers utilize heated air to facilitate the crystallization process, ensuring uniform heating and effective moisture removal from PET materials. In contrast, IRD Crystallizers use infrared radiation for targeted heating, promoting rapid crystallization while reducing energy consumption. Both types aim to enhance the quality of PET for applications in packaging and textiles by improving its thermal and mechanical properties.</p></p>
