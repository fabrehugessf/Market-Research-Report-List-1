<p><strong>The X-ray Systems for Industrial Inspection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Radiography (DR) Type</li><li>Computed Tomography (CT) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>X-ray systems for industrial inspection primarily include two market types: Digital Radiography (DR) and Computed Tomography (CT). Digital Radiography utilizes digital detectors to produce immediate high-quality images, allowing for rapid inspection and analysis of materials and components. In contrast, Computed Tomography offers 3D imaging by capturing multiple 2D X-ray images from various angles, providing detailed internal views of complex structures. Both technologies enhance defect detection and material evaluation, ensuring higher accuracy and efficiency in industrial applications.</p></p>
