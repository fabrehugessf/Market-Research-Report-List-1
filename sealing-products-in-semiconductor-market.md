EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer) is favored for its durability and weather resistance. PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) is renowned for its low friction and non-stick properties. Other sealing materials may include specific thermoplastics or custom compounds, tailored for distinct semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
