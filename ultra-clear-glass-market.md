<p><strong>The Ultra-Clear Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolled Glass</li><li>Float Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra-Clear Glass market is primarily categorized into Rolled Glass and Float Glass. Rolled Glass is produced by passing molten glass through rollers, creating a uniform thickness, suitable for applications requiring specific dimensions. Float Glass, on the other hand, is made by floating molten glass on tin, resulting in a highly flat and transparent product. Both types are prized for their clarity and minimal impurities, making them ideal for various applications in architecture, automotive, and electronics industries.</p></p>
