<p><p>The circular saw market is primarily divided into two types: corded and cordless circular saws. Corded circular saws are powered by electricity and typically provide consistent power for extended use, making them ideal for heavy-duty tasks. In contrast, cordless circular saws operate on rechargeable batteries, offering greater mobility and convenience for outdoor or remote work without the hassle of cords. Both types cater to varying user needs, from professional contractors to DIY enthusiasts, emphasizing efficiency and portability in cutting applications.</p></p>
