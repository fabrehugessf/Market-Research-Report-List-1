<p><p>The prescription safety glasses market is primarily categorized into two types: glass safety eyewear and polycarbonate safety eyewear. Glass safety eyewear offers superior optical clarity and scratch resistance, making it suitable for environments where durability is essential. However, it can be heavier and more prone to shattering. In contrast, polycarbonate safety eyewear is lighter, impact-resistant, and provides UV protection, making it ideal for active work environments. Both types ensure vision protection while accommodating prescription requirements for enhanced visibility and safety.</p></p>
