SCARA robots excel in horizontal movements, perfect for assembly and packaging. Cylindrical robots provide a combination of rotational and linear motion, while parallel robots enable high-speed operations with multiple arms. Other emerging technologies also contribute to advancing automation in this sector.</p></p>
