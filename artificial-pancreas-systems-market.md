<p><strong>The Artificial Pancreas Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Control to Range (CTR) System</li><li>Control to Target (CTT) System</li><li>Threshold Suspended Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Artificial pancreas systems are categorized into three main market types. Control to Range (CTR) systems maintain glucose levels within a safe range, offering flexibility while preventing hypoglycemia. Control to Target (CTT) systems aim to achieve specific glucose targets, enhancing overall glycemic control. Threshold Suspended Devices are designed to automatically suspend insulin delivery when glucose levels fall below a predefined threshold, preventing dangerous lows. Each type contributes to improving diabetes management by automating insulin delivery based on real-time glucose data.</p></p>
