<p><p>The food blanchers market comprises various types, including belt blanchers, drum blanchers, and screw blanchers. Belt blanchers utilize a conveyor belt system to efficiently move food products through hot water or steam for blanching, suitable for larger volumes. Drum blanchers employ a rotating drum to ensure even heating, ideal for bulk processing of irregular-shaped items. Screw blanchers use a screw conveyor to push food through a heating medium, providing continuous processing for specific applications. Each type caters to different operational needs and product specifications.</p></p>
