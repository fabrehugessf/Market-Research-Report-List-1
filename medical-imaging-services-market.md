<p><p>The medical imaging services market encompasses various techniques used for diagnostic purposes. X-rays utilize radiation to visualize internal structures, primarily bones. Molecular imaging focuses on cellular and molecular processes, offering insights into disease progression. Ultrasound imaging employs sound waves to create real-time images of soft tissues and organs, commonly used in pregnancy and cardiology. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) uses powerful magnets and radio waves to generate detailed images of organs and tissues, ideal for soft tissue assessment and neurological conditions.</p></p>
