<p><p>The electromagnetic starter market is primarily categorized into single-phase and three-phase electromagnetic starters. Single-phase electromagnetic starters are designed for smaller motors, typically used in residential and light industrial applications, providing overcurrent protection and simple control. In contrast, three-phase electromagnetic starters cater to larger industrial motors, offering enhanced performance and efficiency for heavy-duty applications. Both types utilize electromagnetic coils to engage and disengage power, ensuring safe operation and protection against electrical faults in motor systems.</p></p>
