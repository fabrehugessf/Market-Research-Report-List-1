<p><p>The electronic photocell market is primarily categorized into two types: Center Cathode Photocells and Center Anode Photocells. Center Cathode Photocells use a cathode-based design, facilitating efficient light sensing and energy management, making them ideal for outdoor lighting applications. In contrast, Center Anode Photocells utilize an anode configuration, offering robust performance and reliability. Both types are essential for automating lighting control systems, enhancing energy efficiency, and contributing to smart city initiatives by optimizing outdoor and street lighting solutions.</p></p>
