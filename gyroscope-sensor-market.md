<p><p>The gyroscope sensor market is categorized into various types based on their sensing technologies. Infrared gyroscopes utilize infrared light to detect rotation and orientation changes. Ultrasonic gyroscopes use sound waves for motion sensing, providing robust accuracy in various environments. Microwave gyroscopes employ microwave signals to track changes, offering high precision for applications like aerospace.
