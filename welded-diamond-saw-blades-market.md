<p><p>Welded diamond saw blades are essential tools in construction and masonry, designed for durability and precision. The market is segmented into high-frequency welding and laser welding types. High-frequency welding involves joining diamond segments to the blade core using electromagnetic energy, resulting in strong bonds and improved heat resistance. Laser welding employs focused laser beams to fuse segments seamlessly, offering superior bond quality and operational efficiency. Both methods enhance blade performance, catering to diverse cutting applications and user needs in various industries.</p></p>
