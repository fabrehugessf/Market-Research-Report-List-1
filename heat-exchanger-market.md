<p><p>The heat exchanger market comprises various types, each serving specific industrial needs. Shell and tube heat exchangers feature cylindrical shells with multiple tubes for efficient heat transfer, widely used in oil refineries. Plate heat exchangers utilize stacked metal plates for compact design and high efficiency, ideal for food processing and HVAC applications. Air-cooled heat exchangers transfer heat using ambient air, suitable for power generation. Other types include spiral and double-pipe heat exchangers, catering to diverse processing requirements across industries.</p></p>
