<p><ul><li>Incomplete Outflow Type</li><li>Complete Outflow Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The positive displacement pipettes market can be categorized into two main types: incomplete outflow type and complete outflow type. Incomplete outflow pipettes retain a small volume of liquid after dispensing, which may lead to inaccuracies in measurements. Conversely, complete outflow pipettes ensure that all liquid is expelled during dispensing, enhancing precision. Both types cater to various laboratory needs, with complete outflow pipettes generally preferred for applications requiring high accuracy and reproducibility, while incomplete outflow types may be suitable for less critical tasks.</p></p>
